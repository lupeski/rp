ReddPlanet Privacy Policy

ReddPlanet does not collect personal information.
Nor does it transmit any personal information,
unless the user explicitly does so.

ReddPlanet uses OAuth with Reddit to sign in.
The user's credentials are never seen by ReddPlanet,
and are only transmitted via Reddit's OAuth sign-in procedure.

Any account information sent from the user's device, is only
sent to Reddit. To see Reddit's Privacy Policy click here.
